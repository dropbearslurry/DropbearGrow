About Us

Built for Growers,
Not Shelves

Dropbear Slurry started with a simple idea: growers deserve access to the same commercial-grade nutrition that professionals use — without the industrial minimums or the marketing fluff.

K-666 and K-333 Plant Tonics are concentrated liquid fertilisers formulated in Queensland with a focus on fast uptake, pH balance, and plant safety. K-666 is built for established plants and heavy feeders. K-333 is our gentler formula — lower potassium silicate, ideal for cuttings, seedlings, and sensitive species.
