About Us

Superior products,
personalised attention

Dropbear Slurry started with the simple idea of making the best plant food we could. From our founders to our front-line workers, we put lots of love, careful thought, and hard science into all we do. We hope you enjoy using our products, and share the experience with others.

Based in Ipswich, Queensland, Dropbear Slurry fertiliser products are designed to penetrate the soil effectively, minimising nutrient runoff and ensuring optimal retention. They provide a balanced mixture of essential plant nutrients to support all stages of growth in both soil and hydroponic farming practices.

We offer fertilisers with a pH of 6.5–6.8 — a range that many plants love. Our formulations cater to the diverse needs of many flowering and food crops, both. Use K-333 on younger plants, and switch to K-666 when it's time to promote flowering and harvest.

Available in 1L & 5L volumes. 10L and above available upon request.

K-333 Plant Tonic

Gentler formula for seedlings, cuttings, and sensitive species. Lower potassium silicate.

K-666 Plant Tonic

Full-strength formula for established plants and heavy feeders. Promotes flowering and harvest.
